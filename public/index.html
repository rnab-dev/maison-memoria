Sustainable by Design

Metal caps are durable, refill-friendly, and designed to last a lifetime. Each cap can be reused, refinished, or passed on — making the bottle a permanent object, not disposable packaging.

By working directly with metal artists, we support regional design collaboration and the preservation of architectural craftsmanship.

"We chose metal because luxury should last — just like memory."

Each artist is credited and compensated for their design contribution.
Craftsmanship is collaboration, not decoration.
