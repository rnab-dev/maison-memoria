# Maison Memoria – Luxury Perfumery  
### L'Oréal Brandstorm 2026  

We built **Maison Memoria** as part of our journey in L'Oréal Brandstorm 2026 — not just as a website, but as an experience.

The idea started with a simple thought:  
*What if a fragrance could hold a memory?*  

From there, we explored India’s sacred spaces, emotions, and cultural depth — and translated that into a digital luxury perfumery that people can feel, not just see.

## 🌟 What this project is  

- A **luxury fragrance brand concept** inspired by India’s spiritual heritage  
- A **digital experience** combining scent, memory, and storytelling  
- A blend of **culture, technology, and emotion**  

This is not just about perfumes — it’s about how places, moments, and feelings stay with us.

---

## ✨ What we built  

- **6 Fragrance Concepts**  
  Each inspired by a sacred Indian location, capturing its essence  

- **AR-based Interaction Idea**  
  Scan the bottle → unlock a story, a vibe, a memory  

- **Memory Journal**  
  A space where users can attach feelings and experiences to fragrances  

- **Complete Website Flow**  
  From exploration → product → cart → checkout  

- **Luxury UI/UX**  
  Smooth animations, golden tones, and a premium feel throughout  

---

## 🎨 The Collection  

- **Banaras Eternal** – Inspired by Varanasi  
- **Golden Mirage** – Inspired by Amritsar  
- **Monk’s Whisper** – Inspired by Bodhgaya  
- **Puri Reverie** – Inspired by Jagannath Puri  
- **Meenakshi Bloom** – Inspired by Madurai  
- **Kedarnath Dawn** – Inspired by Kedarnath  

Each one reflects a different emotion, place, and memory.
